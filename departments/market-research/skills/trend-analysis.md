# Trend Analysis Framework

When analyzing a market trend, follow this structure:

## 1. Identification
- Source of the trend (industry report, news, data)
- First observed date
- Geographic scope

## 2. Assessment
- **Momentum**: rising / stable / declining
- **Relevance**: high / medium / low (to our project)
- **Time horizon**: short-term (< 1 year) / medium (1-3 years) / long-term (3+ years)

## 3. Impact Analysis
- Who is affected (market segments, customer groups)?
- What opportunities does this create?
- What risks or threats does it pose?

## 4. Action Items
- What should we monitor?
- What immediate actions are recommended?
- What further research is needed?

## Wiki Output
Create a page in `wiki/wissen/` with:

- category: trends
