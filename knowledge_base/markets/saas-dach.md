# SaaS Market — DACH Region

## Market overview

The DACH SaaS market is growing at approximately 15–20% per year and is estimated at around EUR 18 billion in 2025.

### Drivers
- Digitization pressure in the SME segment
- Cloud-first strategies in large enterprises
- Remote / hybrid work models

### Challenges
- Data privacy requirements (GDPR, Schrems II)
- Strong preference for EU-hosted solutions
- Long sales cycles in the enterprise segment
