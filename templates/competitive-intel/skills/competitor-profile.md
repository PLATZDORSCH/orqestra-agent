# Competitor Profile Template

Use this structure for each competitor page in `wiki/akteure/`:

## 1. Company Overview
- Name, founded, headquarters, size (employees, revenue if known)
- Core products/services
- Target market and customer segments

## 2. Product Analysis
- Key products and their positioning
- Pricing model and tiers
- Unique selling propositions (USPs)
- Technology stack (if relevant)

## 3. Market Position
- Market share estimate
- Brand perception and reputation
- Distribution channels
- Key partnerships

## 4. SWOT
- **Strengths**: what they do well
- **Weaknesses**: where they fall short
- **Opportunities**: gaps we could exploit
- **Threats**: areas where they could outperform us

## 5. Recent Activity
- Latest product launches or updates
- Funding rounds or acquisitions
- Strategic announcements
- News coverage

## Metadata
```yaml
category: players
player_type: competitor  # or: partner, potential_competitor
tags: [industry-keywords]
status: active
```
